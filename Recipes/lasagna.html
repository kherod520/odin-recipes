Lasagna

Worlds famous Lasagna

This has been considered the best lasagna on the east. People come far and wide to taste this lasagna. It features cheese, pasta sauce, and special seasonings

Anybody that tries this lasagna will be amazed!

Ingredients

  • Pasta sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Eggs
  • Noodles
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Cook Ground Beef
  3. Mix Ground Beef with pasta noodles and cheese
  4. Add Salt and Pepper
  5. Add pasta sauces
  6. Bake lasagna for 90 minutes
